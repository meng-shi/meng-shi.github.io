User Study

Two preliminary studies evaluated Kid Space with children 5 to 8 years old.

Children are playing with Virtual Agent "Oscar"



Study 1 showed that children engaged enthusiastically with the projected character during a math exercise and during physically active games.
Study 2 found that children engaged with a projected agent at a closer distance than with a television. Parents showed a preference for a projected AR agent over an agent on a television or a standard projection. More info can be found in paper.
