A Design Journey about Future

Welcome! This is Meng Shi, a Senior Interaction Designer of Human & AI Research Group, Intel Research Labs, since 2015.

It is a place full of innovation and creativity. We are targeting on 5-10 years future products. My focus is combining human centered design with AI technologies into smart space, such as manufacturing space, child play space, etc. I am also exploring user research and prototyping areas.

Before entering Intel Labs, I got my Master Degree from Carnegie Mellon University, Tangible Interaction Design Program advised by Aisling Kelliher and dual Bachelor Degree from Tsinghua University, Information Design and Economics.
