Laptops: a non-interactive cuboid when closed.
The lifetime of a laptop closed at least 10 hours a day
How can we help user focus, create + connect when closed, to do their most important work?

Can we add a side screen to PC, and using that screen to connect users while laptop's lid is closed?

Challenges

  • Novel concept design with no prior art for determining
  • Content selection and presentation
  • Apply design with multimodal sensing and sense making technology
  • Global collabration and development
